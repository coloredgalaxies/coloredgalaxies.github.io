Merced was a lovely place in which I have made lifelong connections and friendships, however it is a school very limited in terms of opportunity. UC Riverside is much closer to home and more centralized to different networks and medical schools.

I want to be a friendly and familiar face to all seeking mental help, as I aim to attend medical school and go into psychiatry. For now, I am simply a student with interests in gaming and art.
